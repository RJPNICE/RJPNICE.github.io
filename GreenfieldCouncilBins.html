Report a missed bin collection

If your bin has been missed, the quickest way to tell us is online.

Once submitted, your online report will be immediately passed to the collection crew to rearrange your collection. You will need to leave your bin out for when our collection crews return. This is usually within 24 hours.

Before you start

Before reporting a missed bin you should:

  • check when your collection was due
  • check for reported bin collection issues

If you've already reported it

If you've already reported a missed collection, you:

  • do not need to report it again
  • should leave your bins out so we can collect them over the coming week

How to report

You should report a missed collection to us online within 24 hours of your scheduled collection day.
